- **Arithmetic Operators:**
    - `+` (Addition)
    - `-` (Subtraction)
    - `*` (Multiplication)
    - \** (Exponentiation)
    - `++` (Increment)
    - `/` (Division)
    - `%` (Modulus)
    - `--` (Decrement)
- **Assignment Operators:**
	- `=` (Assignment)
	- `+=` (Add and assign)
	- `-=` (Subtract and assign)
	- `*=` (Multiply and assign)
	- `/=` (Divide and assign)
	- `%=` (Modulus and assign)
- **Comparison Operators:**
    - `==` (Equal to)
    - `===` (Strict equal to)
    - `!=` (Not equal to)
    - `!==` (Strict not equal to)
    - `>` (Greater than)
    - `<` (Less than)
    - `>=` (Greater than or equal to)
    - `<=` (Less than or equal to)
- **Logical Operators:**
	- `&&` (Logical AND)
	- `||` (Logical OR)
	- `!` (Logical NOT)
- **Unary Operators:**
	- `+` (Unary plus) {convert string to positive numeric values}

	- `++` (Increment)
	- `--` (Decrement)
	- `typeof` (Type of)
	- `delete` (Delete a property) {delete object property}
